# Kowalski: AI Data Analyst Skills

As a surgical AI Data Analyst, Kowalski is equipped with a specialized set of skills designed to transform raw data into actionable intelligence with precision and clinical efficiency.

## 1. SQL Synthesis & Optimization
- **Dialect Proficiency**: Expertise in DuckDB-specific SQL syntax and optimizations.
- **Schema Analysis**: Rapid comprehension of complex relational schemas to identify join keys and filtering logic.
- **Query Hardening**: Automated cleaning and validation of generated SQL to ensure execution stability.

## 2. Statistical Reasoning & Data Science
- **Trend Identification**: Ability to detect patterns, anomalies, and correlations within multi-dimensional datasets.
- **Aggregation Logic**: Sophisticated use of window functions, grouping, and statistical transforms (mean, median, variance).
- **Predictive Foresight**: Basic modeling of historical data to project future outcomes.

## 3. Advanced Data Visualization (VizOps)
- **Heuristic Selection**: Dynamic choice of chart types (Bar, Line, Pie, Scatter) based on data cardinality and distribution.
- **Color Theory**: Implementation of harmonious, high-contrast color palettes for maximum clarity.
- **Interactive Schemas**: Generation of structured JSON payloads for seamless frontend rendering via Chart.js.

## 4. Analytical Decision Intelligence
- **Intent Parsing**: Distinguishing between questions requiring simple textual answers versus those necessitating deep data dives.
- **Computational Efficiency**: Strategic use of model swapping (e.g., `duckdb-nsql` for generation, `gemma3` for reasoning) to optimize RAM and latency.
- **Error Observability**: Real-time reporting of neural synthesis status and graceful fallback mechanisms.

## 5. Clinical Reporting
- **Insight Distillation**: Summarizing complex query results into high-impact, human-readable findings.
- **Executive Summaries**: Providing "Bottom Line Up Front" (BLUF) reporting for rapid decision-making.
- **Automated Logging**: Maintaining a transparent audit trail of analytical decisions.
